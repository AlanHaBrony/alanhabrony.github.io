Is Digitalization the future

2024-04-27 18:36

Gradually with PC and mobile devices, Internet has reformed and even substituted some part of human life, such as archives, all kinds of data or files, social connection and more social scenes are being experimented to digitalize even as conducting surgery. 2024-04-27 18:56

However, only the certification necessitiates the academic institutes, while learning and opportunities are all not exclusive. 2024-04-27 19:29

However, the scope of digitalization is still limited in forms. Well the most important part, human brain, is still full of mysteries. Also relatively much easier the smell and tactile sense are far from common use.
