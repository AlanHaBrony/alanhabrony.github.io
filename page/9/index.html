Meaning of sid

AH! Now I finally find the meaning of the sid. Following the six-figure segmnet of date, I found actually it is the whole timestamp of, I suppose, the time when the picture is capture. And the numbers before timestamp, I guess, is related to the city with some encoding which I haven’t found any clue.

But just the timestamp is very helpful. Firstly, it could tell the information about the image. Moreover, this provides a method to iterate the specific place. Since with the timestamps of two adjacent places, just iterate the sid could make sure the places within the time segment selected are captured. This is very helpful for the deviation of choosing coordinates.

At first I wanted to use “interpretation”, while after checking its main meaning “translating any language to another orally”, and “explanation: explaining something - giving more information about something”, I just chose “meaning”.

2024-03-17 10:53
This is the reason I rather value timestamps, they can tell the stories and give data order to show the whole picture.
