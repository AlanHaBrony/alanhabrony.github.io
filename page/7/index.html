So I changed to old devices.

  1. Chromeboxes with 2955u
    These devices are cheap and just enough for linux to running scripts.
  2. Industrial PC of small manufacturers
    These devices are lack of information and the hardwares are probably under poor conditions.

I also need portability and I had no idea about the size of cases, so I used my laptop’s size as comparision to decide around 180mm*180mm is suitable.
