Some examples of Graph in different context:

  1. Graphs in Computer Science, which I think of is applying graphs by its nature as models of the individuals and its relationship and then conducting Algorithms.
  2. Networks, which I thought while reading, is more practical with clear restrictions in the form of the graphs. As a result, in my opinion, Networks became a object on its own with few properties of general graphs. So I think Networks, such as in EE, NN, Internet and Biological Networks.
  3. Probabilistic Graphical Model, which I thought was mainly a type of probabilistic model using graphs. So I didn’t continute to read when I found its textbooks are in fact of Probability theory and Statistics, other than the Graph theory itself I was looking for.
  4. Dynamic Networks, which I thought was interesting for the dynamic property but by its nature a practical model in Applied Mathematics.
  5. Knowledge Graph, Social Networks, etc.
