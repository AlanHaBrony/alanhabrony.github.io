2024-05-09 20:00
So some notes are:

  1. Many OR problems involve Graphs and OR is diverse.
  2. OR could be under different departments, such as Economics and Management in Bussiness school, Mathematics and Computer Science.
  3. So one can choose research topics with a lot of freedom as a Professor, while a PhD is still a academic program.
  4. Finding a PhD advisor is important.
  5. Most OR/IE PhD programs do faculty-oriented admissions and therefore finding an advisor whose work one is interested in is important, since one will work with the advisor.
