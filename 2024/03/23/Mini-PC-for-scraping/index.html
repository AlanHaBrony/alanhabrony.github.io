Till now I just have my Windows desktop and a 2-core ECS with Ubuntu running scraping scripts. I need to keep my desktop charging and without power off for a 24h scraping. But sometimes I may need to take my desktop, so I started to look for a Mini PC for scraping.
I want to use just GNU and not to wast the price. I don’t know whether 2-core machines are enough, the storage surely should above 40G and larger is better. So I firstly searched about N100, N5095, J4125, etc. Most posts are about router but I dont’t need WANs and LANs. Then I saw a Mini PC with N5095, 16G RAM and 512G storage.
